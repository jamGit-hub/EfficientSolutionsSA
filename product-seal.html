Seal Types We Supply

Lineproof Engineering manufactures a comprehensive range of mechanical seals — each designed for precision performance under demanding industrial conditions. We bring these to the Saudi market with local support.

Single Mechanical Seal

Standard industrial pump sealing solution — reliable, cost-effective, and easy to maintain. Wave-spring design ensures consistent face load across a broad shaft size range.

Best for: General pump applications

Dual Cartridge Seal

ANSI-standard dual cartridge configuration providing double-layer sealing for hazardous or critical process fluids. Zero adjustment required on installation.

Best for: Hazardous & critical fluids

Metal Bellows Seal

All-metal welded bellows design — eliminates elastomers for extreme temperature applications. Suitable for cryogenic service down to −200 °C and high-temperature up to +400 °C.

Best for: Extreme temperature service

Dry-Running Seal

Engineered for gas or near-vapour applications where liquid lubrication is unavailable. Maintains a controlled gas film enabling zero-leakage operation in compressors and blowers.

Best for: Gas / vapour service

Reactor / Agitator Seal

Double-acting seal purpose-built for top- or side-entry agitators in reactors. Handles angular misalignment and radial deflection while maintaining containment under variable pressures.

Best for: Reactors & agitators

Split Mechanical Seal

Split-design for in-place installation without dismantling pipework or bearings — drastically reducing maintenance downtime on large pumps in continuous-process facilities.

Best for: Minimal-downtime maintenance
