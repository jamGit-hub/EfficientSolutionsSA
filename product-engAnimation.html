How It Works

From Concept to Final Animation

A streamlined four-step process — coordinated by Efficient Solutions between you and Mechanim's studio team.

01

Brief & Scope

We gather your CAD files, use-case objectives, and target audience to define the animation scope.

02

3D Modelling

Mechanim's engineers build or refine the 3D model with engineering precision, ensuring technical accuracy.

03

Animation & Review

Motion, lighting, and rendering are finalized. You review and approve at each milestone.

04

Delivery & Support
